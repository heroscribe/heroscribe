Hero Quest

RULES OF PLAY

The Board

The board consists of squares that are grouped into different areas. Rooms are areas limited by walls (the white lines). Passages are the areas around and between the rooms. There are 4 outer passages around the edge of the board, 4 inner passages around the central room, and 4 traverse passages connecting the inner and outer passages. Passages are limited by walls, the edge of the board or junctions. Junctions are squares that belong to 2 passages, like the 4 corners of the board.

Revealing an Area

Areas are either covered or revealed. At the beginning of the game all areas are covered. When an area is revealed, Zargon places all items shown for that area in the map, except secret items. Zargon places items inside the area, like monsters and furniture, and items on the boundaries of the area, like doors and blocked square tiles. Once revealed, an area stays revealed until the end of the game.

An area is revealed when:

  • an open door is placed on the boundaries of the area
  • a figure controlled by a character player is about to enter the area
The former happens when a character player opens a door, or when Zargon or one of his monsters opens a door if allowed by the quest. The latter happens when a figure wants to step on a junction, is teleported into a covered area, or is able to pass through walls. Zargon interrupts his movement and reveals the room before the figure moves on.

The Hero's Turn

As a hero, when it is your turn you may move and perform an action. Possible actions are:

  • attack
  • cast a spell
  • search
  • special actions described in the quest notes
You can move and perform the action in any order.

If you are in possession of items that allow you to perform actions additionally, you can do so on your turn before or after your movement, or before or after your regular action.

If you are in possession of items that allow you to use them at any time, you can do so even if it is not your turn. If you use such an item or spell on your turn, you can even interrupt your movement and regular action to do so.

If an items tells you to do something immediately, the active player's turn is interrupted until you have done so. If you are the active player and use such an item, this does not influence your ability to perform your regular action this turn.

Casting Spells

At the beginning of each quest, you may take as many sets of spell cards as indicated on your character sheet. If more than one player

You may cast a spell on yourself or on a target located either:

  • in your area
  • in an area adjacent to your square
  • on a square adjacent to your area

Discard the spell after use.

Potions

You may use potions at any time. Discard the potion after use.

Heroes

All heroes roll 2 dice in defence and 2 dice for movement.

Barbarian

You are the Barbarian, the greatest fighter of all. You may roll 3 dice in attack.

Dwarf

You are the Dwarf. You may roll 2 dice in attack. You may roll for defence when suffering damage from traps.

Elf

You are the Elf. You may roll 2 dice in attack. You may take the spells from one school at the beginning of the quest.

Wizard

You are the Wizard, a master of magic. You may roll 1 die in attack. You may take the spells from three school at the beginning of the quest.
